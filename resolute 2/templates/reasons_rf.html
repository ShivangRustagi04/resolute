Reasons to Choose Random Forest Classifier

  • Ensemble Learning: Random Forest is an ensemble learning method that builds multiple decision trees and merges them together to get a more accurate and stable prediction.
  • Handles Non-linearity: Random Forest can capture complex non-linear relationships in the data.
  • Feature Importance: Random Forest provides a feature importance score, helping to identify which features are most influential in making predictions.
  • Robust to Overfitting: Random Forest is less prone to overfitting compared to individual decision trees.
  • Works well with Large Datasets: Random Forest can efficiently handle large datasets and high-dimensional feature spaces.
